I've known God for seven years, and my life has been completely transformed. This journey has given me meaning and direction.

最初是因为婚姻关系，经历了很多挑战。通过朋友认识了上帝，阅读圣经让我找到了内心的平安，这七年的信仰之旅让我成长了许多。

It started with marriage challenges. Through a friend's introduction to God and reading the Bible, I found inner peace. These seven years of faith have helped me grow tremendously.

2. 我喜欢美发 / My Passion for Hairstyling

我从事美发行业20年，从十九岁开始接触的时候就很喜欢。剪发经常给我一种心流的体验。

I've been in the hairstyling industry for 20 years, falling in love with it since I was 19. Cutting hair often brings me into a state of flow.

喜欢做头发本身，纯粹养头发，设计发型，研究发型带来的美。不管是长发、齐肩、披肩、快板、挑染。

I love everything about hair - from nurturing healthy hair to designing styles and exploring the beauty in different cuts and colors.
